About Me

My name is Joaquin and I am an audiovisual artist.
I studied Electronic Arts and Web Programming; both Technology as well as Art are areas that I am passionate about since I was very young.
I worked as video editor and sound engineer for a Production Company of entertainment and information content during three years. I was in charge of multiple projects with different formats like interviews, reviews, videogames, tiktoks, reels, entertainment and streams, among others.
I have edited videos for various content creators on both Youtube and Instagram; and I have recorded sound for various Podcast and On Demand shows.
Today, due to my studies and professional development, I have the knowledge and the tools to easily tackle any project I propose to undertake.
In my free time, I dedicate myself to producing music, taking photos and filming videos in order to create small artistic montages. On the other hand, I really enjoy cooking, cycling and practicing yoga.
